Calm Pop is a simple stress relief game built around quick taps, soft motion, and slow breathing.

Tap floating bubbles before they drift away, build a combo, and follow the breathing bar at the bottom of the screen. Each round is short and lightweight, making it easy to take a quick break.

Features:
- Relaxing bubble popping gameplay
- Short timed rounds
- Combo scoring
- Gentle animated background
- Breathing guide for a calmer pace
